import { Box, Text } from 'rebass'

<Box sx={{whiteSpace: 'nowrap'}}>
  <Text>Above my small bed higher than I can quite</Text>
  <Text>reach standing on a wobbling chair</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>tiptoes an unsteady dance of</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>stretching arms and air currents,</Text>
  <Text pl={3} mb={3}>fingers grasping.</Text>

  <Text>Haphazardly tacked across my ceiling</Text>
  <Text>lies a nest of tiny green-glowing stars</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>their light fit to illuminate only</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>themselves and any piece of</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>an idea projected</Text>
  <Text pl={3} mb={3}>through them.</Text>

  <Text>I remember once</Text>
  <Text>using the curled ropes of your hair</Text>
  <Text>to connected them together. You asked why</Text>
  <Text>and I told you the story of the star scorpion</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>whose barbed tail is made of the</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>hottest bits of sun fire and whose</Text>
  <Text pl={3} mb={3}>eyes heavy silver neutron stars.</Text>

  <Text>And once the last big light flickers and</Text>
  <Text>coughs dust across the night sky</Text>
  <Text>he will wave pincers, claws a net of gravity</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>catching the last meaty bits</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>of the universe, comets</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>crushed and then dissolved by</Text>
  <Text pl={3} mb={3}>ancient poison.</Text>

  <Text>But you did not quite follow as my hands</Text>
  <Text>traced a hundred shapes in the air,</Text>
  <Text>and that might have been best, since the</Text>
  <Text>false light had faded from the roof and from your</Text>
  <Text>eyes. I sighed and let the air escape skyward,</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>carrying bits of my dreams along</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>toward his exact jaws so that</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>one day my breath, too, would be</Text>
  <Text pl={3}>compacted and devoured.</Text>
  <Text>And eventually, from the quivering soup</Text>
  <Text>of the bits uneaten, a piece might start</Text>
  <Text>to spin and spin in the receding silence.</Text>
</Box>
