import { Text } from 'rebass';

<Text>If I bend my neck back just enough</Text>
<Text pl={3}>each wrinkled crease a slithering earthquake of skin</Text>
<Text>I can see where the tallest spruce</Text>
<Text>begins to pierce the bottom edge of the sun</Text>
<Text pl={3}>in just a moment its yolk will run out</Text>
<Text pl={3} mb={3}>and be absorbed by the softest bits of sky.</Text>

<Text>And I know I could chase the sunset,</Text>
<Text>refuse to be pressed into that final chill of night</Text>
<Text pl={3}>like a pen forced to black ink.</Text>
<Text>I would start a slow jog</Text>
<Text>bounding past withered lawns,</Text>
<Text>doors shut against the rising dark.</Text>
<Text>Past the end of day and my</Text>
<Text>dog’s last, faint howl.</Text>
<Text>But I know I couldn’t keep up</Text>
<Text pl={3}>my legs are kindling burned too quickly.</Text>
<Text>Before I’d have to turn and face</Text>
<Text>the blade of each jagged shadow</Text>
<Text>I might steal a car</Text>
<Text pl={3}>adrenaline a tiny fire</Text>
<Text pl={3} mb={3}>in each quivering finger.</Text>

<Text>I would scream into the last rays of light,</Text>
<Text>windshield catching the sun while</Text>
<Text>it makes war with bugs and bits of earth.</Text>
<Text>But even then my engine would cough and wheeze</Text>
<Text pl={3}>there is no cheetah that can run so</Text>
<Text pl={3} mb={3}>fast, so long.</Text>

<Text>So I would have to hop on a jet</Text>
<Text>and explode into the air.</Text>
<Text>And there above the world</Text>
<Text>I could stay suspended on bits of string</Text>
<Text>hung from wispy clouds forever</Text>
<Text pl={3}>sunlight woven to pieces</Text>
<Text pl={3}>of morning and afternoon</Text>
<Text>And I might even have used enough energy</Text>
<Text mb={3}>to trick myself into thinking I had lived.</Text>

<Text>But instead, oh</Text>
<Text>instead I will stay on this porch swing</Text>
<Text>and shiver just a little.</Text>
<Text>I will count each star</Text>
<Text>and I will connect them into a scorpion,</Text>
<Text mb={3}>a bear and a small silver ladybug.</Text>

<Text>Eventually my chin will fall</Text>
<Text>and fuse with my chest, my eyelids will rattle shut.</Text>
<Text>And these three will pull themselves from the night sky</Text>
<Text>drifting down to dance just beneath my skin</Text>
<Text pl={3}>their looping tracery a ward of heart</Text>
<Text pl={3}>that I will take with me when I</Text>
<Text pl={3}>visit the deeper parts of dusk.</Text>
